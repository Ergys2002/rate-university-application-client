Who We Are

The Polytechnic University of Tirana is the oldest and the second largest university in Albania. It was founded in 1951 and now has approximately 10,000 students, who come from Albania, Kosova, Montenegro, and North Macedonia. The university is fully accredited by the Albanian Public Agency for Accreditation of Higher Education (APAAHE).
